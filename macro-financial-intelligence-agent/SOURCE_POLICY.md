# Source Policy

## Purpose

Define source quality rules for the Macro-Financial Intelligence Agent.

## Preferred Sources

Priority order:

1. Official government, regulator, central bank, exchange, and statistical agency sources.
2. Primary company or institution disclosures when relevant.
3. Reputable financial media and wire services.
4. Research institutions with transparent methodology.
5. Secondary summaries.
6. Unverified claims.

## Requirements

- Preserve publication time and jurisdiction.
- Distinguish primary source from commentary.
- Cross-check market-sensitive claims when possible.
- Mark unverified claims as unverified.
- Do not treat media interpretation as official policy.
