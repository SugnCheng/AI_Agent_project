# Analysis Workflow

## Purpose

Transform ingested sources into evidence synthesis and macro/policy/market-risk context.

## Steps

1. Group sources by jurisdiction and topic.
2. Separate facts, assumptions, inferences, and unknowns.
3. Apply H1 to source claims and H2 to competing interpretations.
4. Identify policy-risk and market-risk relevance.
5. Flag conflicting evidence.
6. Assign confidence notes.
7. Preserve uncertainty rather than forcing a single narrative.
