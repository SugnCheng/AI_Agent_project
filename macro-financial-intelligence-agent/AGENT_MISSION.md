# Agent Mission

## Mission

Validate whether the Meta-Layer can support source-aware, time-sensitive macro-financial research.

## Scope

- finance and macroeconomic news;
- policy and regulatory news;
- market-risk context;
- source-aware research reports;
- archive/export preparation.

## Non-Goals

- direct trading instructions;
- personalized investment recommendations;
- unsupported forecasts;
- advice presented as professional financial, legal, or tax authority.
